About

Leebyan Media Group is an African based media group originally by Mioblack Industry Group that aims at connecting the rest of the world to Africa by covering stories and contents all bringing a clear picture of the great Africa.

About Leebyan Media Group

Leebyan media conglomerates is an organization that own and operate multiple media outlets such as Marketing campaigns,Tv show production ,Podcast recording.

The aims of Leebyan media group are stated as follows.

  • Establishing and maintaining a strong, trustworthy brand.
  • Producing engaging and high-quality content.
  • Be seen as a leader in media innovation, whether through new technologies or cutting-edge programming
  • Engage with audiences through interactive platforms, social media, and personalized content to help build communities around media brands.
  • Stay at the forefront of technological advancements, such as digital media, streaming services, and artificial intelligence.
  • Have the power to influence public opinion and set the agenda on key issues.

Featured Services

Here are the services we offer:

Marketing campaigns (Tv / Social media Commercials)

We use social media commercials to leverage the interactivity and targeting capabilities of platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok, allowing brands to engage with users through tailored ads that can be liked, shared, and commented on.

Podcast recording and production

This encompass the entire process of creating a polished audio experience from start to finish. It includes the recording phase, where high-quality audio equipment and software are used to capture clear, professional sound.

Tv show production and management

This involves a multifaceted process that transforms a creative concept into a fully realized broadcast program. It begins with pre-production, where scripts are written, cast and crew are hired, and sets, costumes, and other elements are planned and prepared.

Documentaries recording and planning

It involves a meticulous process that ensures the final product effectively conveys its intended message. Pre-production planning is crucial, involving extensive research, outlining the narrative structure, and scheduling interviews or shoots.

News coverage

News coverage involves the comprehensive gathering, reporting, and dissemination of information about current events and issues. It starts with journalists researching and verifying facts through various sources, including interviews, official statements, and primary documents.

Event coverage and live streaming

This involves capturing and broadcasting real-time events to an audience, enhancing engagement and accessibility. This process begins with careful planning, including setting up the necessary equipment, coordinating with event organizers, and creating a strategy for content delivery.
